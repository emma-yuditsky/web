Who Are They?

The Front Bottoms is a band that formed circa 2006. They are now Brian Sella and Mat Uychich. Brian Sella does vocals and plays the guitar, and Mat Uychich plays the drums and bullhorn. Their genre is described as Folk punk,indie rock,alternative rock, pop, and emo.

Their Website

History

The band started out in 2006 playing in New Jersey where they started out. The original members where Brian Sella and Mat Uychich(They are also the current members), and for a time Brain Uychich who remained an active member until he left to focus on school in 2010. They toured often and had several member come an go since then. They have released many songs and music videos and in 2015 they were signed to Fueled By Ramen.
